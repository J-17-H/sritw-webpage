Sumathi Reddy Institute of Technology for Women (SRITW), located in Warangal, is a reputed autonomous institution affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad. The college is dedicated to empowering women by providing high-quality engineering education and promoting innovation.

Vision

To create empowered women engineers with strong technical skills and leadership qualities.

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality education.
  • Encourage innovation and creativity.
  • Support student research and skill development.
